Call Of Duty: Black Ops 3

Campaign

In Campaign mode, you will be on a mission that takes place in 2052. There are many things you might find in the game very furturistic. You will experience the ability to wallrun, jump boost off the ground, and much more abilities that you can think of in the game as long as it is futuristic. There are many enemies in the game as you play through more of what you are going to experience. There will be also fighting robots and destoying them, hacking and breaching walls, shooting people in slow-motion, and much more as you play through the game.

Multiplayer

In multiplayer mode, there are multiple characters(also known as specialists) you can play as in the game, and you can also use their special abilities and weapons.

    Outrider(Allesandra Castillo)

    The Outrider(also known as Allesandra Castillo) is a female character who has an ability which is called the Sparrow. Alessandra Castillo is a Hispanic woman who is involved with the BSF(Brazilian Special Forces). She is specialized in taking out enemies from far distances. The Sparrow is a bow where you can shoot the enemies with it. The cool thing about her weapon is that when the arrow hits a target, the bow will make a huge explosion.The Outrider's other ability is called "vision pulse."When you use her ability, she can see through walls and also the targets which are the enemies.

    Ruin(Donnie Walsh)

    The Ruin(also known as Donnie Walsh)is a male character that was born into a military family and was raised in a tough neighborhood. Donnie was raised headstrong and fearless, as he voluntarily accepted cybernetic enhancements to become a far more efficient killer. He joined the army straight out of high school, and Donnie is an infantry soldier through and through which made his battlefield his second home. His specialist weapon is called gravity spikes that allow him to use spikes, jump up and make destruction of whatever is surrounding him. His specialist ability is called overdrive that allows him to increase his speed, and make him fast like Sonic.

    Prophet(David Wilkes)

    Wilkes is a male character who got his first set of cybernetic augmentations while serving in the BEC(British Engineering Corps). 5 years and many upgrades later, he became the embodiment of the cutting edge of human weaponry on the battlefield, using his superior technology to disrupt opponent's tactics before moving in for the kill. David's specialist weapon called the tempest, is a huge electrogun that shoots lightning bolts at enemies that are targeted, making the enemy die from electrocution. If any other enemies stand near the electrocuted person, the bolt will pass it on to person near it, making the bolt sort of contagious. David's specialist weapon called glitch can teleport you back to where any other position you left off with.

    Battery(Erin Baker)

    The Battery(also known as Erin Baker)is a female character has been following in a family tradition of over a century of military service, and watching her brothers enter the US Army one-by-one, she finally graduated high school and enrolled herself. Driven to excel by her ongoing rivalry with her brothers, she volunteered for Airborne School, before crushing the Ranger Assessment and Selection Program. Heavily armored, she relentlessly operates ahead of the frontlines, nimbly traversing urban environments, as she eliminates HVTs with salvoes of advanced high-explosive munitions. Baker's specialist weapon called the war machine, shoots multiple grenades at once when targeting an enemy. Baker's specialist ability called kinetic armor, gives herself a protective armor sheild with more defense, and allows her armor to take more bullets in order for her to be eliminated.
