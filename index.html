Call Of Duty: Black Ops 3

Campaign

In Campaign mode, you will be on a mission that takes place in 2052. There are many things you might find in the game very furturistic. You will experience the ability to wallrun, jump boost off the ground, and much more abilities that you can think of in the game as long as it is futuristic. There are many enemies in the game as you play through more of what you are going to experience. There will be also fighting robots and destoying them, hacking and breaching walls, shooting people in slow-motion, and much more as you play through the game.

Multiplayer

In multiplayer mode, there are multiple characters(also known as specialists) you can play as in the game, and you can also use their special abilities and weapons.

    Outrider(Allesandra Castillo)

    The Outrider(also known as Allesandra Castillo) is a female character who has an ability which is called the Sparrow. Alessandra Castillo is a Hispanic woman who is involved with the BSF(Brazilian Special Forces). She is specialized in taking out enemies from far distances. The Sparrow is a bow where you can shoot the enemies with it. The cool thing about her weapon is that when the arrow hits a target, the bow will make a huge explosion.The Outrider's other ability is called "vision pulse."When you use her ability, she can see through walls and also the targets which are the enemies. In order to play as the Outrider, you must level up to earn an unlock token, and you will able to unlock her with your unlock token.

    Ruin(Donnie Walsh)

    The Ruin(also known as Donnie Walsh)is a male character that was born into a military family and was raised in a tough neighborhood. Donnie was raised headstrong and fearless, as he voluntarily accepted cybernetic enhancements to become a far more efficient killer. He joined the army straight out of high school, and Donnie is an infantry soldier through and through which made his battlefield his second home. His specialist weapon is called gravity spikes that allow him to use spikes, jump up and make destruction of whatever is surrounding him. His specialist ability is called overdrive that allows him to increase his speed, and make him fast like Sonic. In order to play as Ruin, you must level up to earn an unlock token, and you will be able to use this character.

    Prophet(David Wilkes)

    Wilkes is a male character who got his first set of cybernetic augmentations while serving in the BEC(British Engineering Corps). 5 years and many upgrades later, he became the embodiment of the cutting edge of human weaponry on the battlefield, using his superior technology to disrupt opponent's tactics before moving in for the kill. David's specialist weapon called the tempest, is a huge electrogun that shoots lightning bolts at enemies that are targeted, making the enemy die from electrocution. If any other enemies stand near the electrocuted person, the bolt will pass it on to person near it, making the bolt sort of contagious. David's specialist weapon called glitch can teleport you back to where any other position you left off with. This is the specialist character you play first in multiplayer.

    Battery(Erin Baker)

    The Battery(also known as Erin Baker)is a female character has been following in a family tradition of over a century of military service, and watching her brothers enter the US Army one-by-one, she finally graduated high school and enrolled herself. Driven to excel by her ongoing rivalry with her brothers, she volunteered for Airborne School, before crushing the Ranger Assessment and Selection Program. Heavily armored, she relentlessly operates ahead of the frontlines, nimbly traversing urban environments, as she eliminates HVTs with salvoes of advanced high-explosive munitions. Baker's specialist weapon called the war machine, shoots multiple grenades at once when targeting an enemy. Baker's specialist ability called kinetic armor, gives herself a protective armor sheild with more defense, and allows her armor to take more bullets in order for her to be eliminated. In order to play as the Battery, you must level up to earn an unlock token, and use that unlock token to unlock the character.

    Nomad(Tavo Rojas)

    The Nomad(also known as Tavo Rojas)is an ex-member of the Elite Colombian Rapid Deployment Force. Tavo is an expert in jungle warfare, able to blend into the forests and survive off of the land almost indefinitely. As sole survivor, he seeks retribution for his fallen friends, using his skills to turn the environment against his enemies. The Nomad's specialist weapon called H.I.V.E., is a futuristic weapon that fires pod traps that releases nano drones onto the enemies. The Nomad's specialist aability called Rejack, allows him to recover the position of his death. In order to play as the Nomad, you must meet the level requirement which is level 28.

    Seraph(He Zhen-Zhen)

    The only child of a feared officer of the 54 Immortals, He Zhen-Zhen(Seraph)was raised within the ranks of the Singapore Quarantine Zone's ruling criminal combine. She proved to be an excellent and dedicated pupil, learning the traditions and honored lineage of an organization while pursing martial art training with her single-minded determination. At the age of 17, He Zhen-Zhen was elevated to the rank of Enforcer, one of the few who police the combine from within while ruthlessly executing the will of the 54I wherever it might be required. The Seraph's specialist weapon called Annihilator is a one-shot kill revolver with infinite range and incredible penetration power. When her specialist ability called Combat Focus is activated, all score earned will activate a bonus multiplier towards scorestreaks. In order to unlock the Seraph, you must meet the level requirement which is level 22.

    Reaper(Experimental War Robot-115)

    The product of a cutting-edge military R and D project is to supplement special Operations soldiers in the field. Experimental War Robot-115 was one of the single unit of advanced prototype combat robots running the most sophisticated AI software ever seen. After many politically embarrassing mishaps and budget overruns, the project was rejected and many of the prototypes stripped for pieces. Nobody knows why it attacks or who, if anyone, is following its commands. The Reaper's specialist weapon is called Scythe which causes his arm to turn into a quad barrel mini-gun. The mini-gun must be spun while using the aim button for a couple of seconds until it can be fired. The Reaper will move slower than usual while the mini-gun is being spun or fired. When its specialist ability called Psychosis is activated, the Reaper will create three decoys. If you don't know the decoy looks like, it tends to be a glowing blue color. As the decoys turn a glowing blue, the Reaper can walk forward and mock-fire at nearby enemies, and will also activate enemy equipment and becomes targeted by scorestreaks. In order to unlock this character, you must meet the level requirement which is level 34.

    Spectre

    The Spectre has an unknown gender identity and unknown birthdate. The designation itself is a codename agreed upon by the various agencies that have pooled their intelligence on unidentified assassinations, inferring its existence based on either proof discoovered at the scene or from fragmentary reports given by shaken bystanders. Spectre's victims have been slain with surgically precise mid-line stab wounds with rarely any signals of hesitation. The Spectre's specialist weapon calle Ripper are twin, sharp blades that gives extended melee lunge against enemies. When the Spectre's specialist ability called Active Camo is activated,it allows the Spectre to become invisible. If the Spectre attacks while it is invisible, then the result will de-actiivate the camo making him or her become able to be seen. In order to unlock the Spectre, you must mee the level requirement which is level 40.

    Firebreak(Krystof Hejek)

    Krystof Hejek is a battlefield problem solver that is heavily armored with a powerful arsenal of weaponry. He effectively resolves a variety of problems over and over again using his favorite tool which is fire of course. Whether it's direct one-on-one confrontation or the strategic denial of areas, there's no questioning Hejek's utility, even if the results of his work can be painful to the stomach. Krystof seems to have an understanding with his fire. The only time he lights up occurs when his enemies do. The Firebreak's specialist weapon called the Purifier, is a flamethrower that can light up enemies on fire who will continuosly still be burning over time. When his specialist ability called Heat Wave is activated, he will emit a huge heat blast that stuns the targets and wrecks hostile equipment within its range, even including upcoming projectiles. In order to unlock the Firebreak, you must reach at level 48.

    Blackjack(Bonus Specialist)

    Blackjack is the new added specialist of the Call Of Duty Black Ops 3 update. There is now 10 specialists in the Call Of Duty Blacks Ops 3 game. The only way you can earn Blackjack is by completing weekly contracts. Once you have completed the weekly contract, you will have a chance to play as Blackjack for 60 minutes. Blckjack's specialist weapon is called Rogue that allows Blackjack to copy the same specialist weapon from the last enemy player killed. Blackjack's specialist ability called Gambler, allows Blackjack to fill in his power meter faster, awarding him with a random specialist weapon.
