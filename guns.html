More Multiplayer

Guns And Perks

In this section of multiplayer, I will be showing you the weapons and the added weapons on the game. I will also be showing you the perks. There are many sorts of guns such as sub-machine guns, assault rifles, snipers, light machine guns, and shotguns. There are also secondary weapons such as pistols, rocket launchers, and melee weapons. I will be showing you all of the picture of the weapon, the name of the weapon, the attachments that can be added to the weapon, and the category of which weapon is which.

The Kuda is a fully automatic weapon, and is also one of the weapons you'll start with when you play your first or second game. It doesn't need a specific weapon to use and it could be equipped to your loadout immediately. It has moderate damage, and has decent range while at it. It also has a medium-paced firing rate, and fine accuracy. The Kuda after Black Ops 3 was made, was one of the best weapons in the game.
